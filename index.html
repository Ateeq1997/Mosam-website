Our Programs and Offerings

Quran Learning

We provide comprehensive Quran learning programs, from beginner to advanced, focusing on recitation, memorization, and understanding.

Qaida Learning

Start your Quranic journey with Qaida learning, where students master the foundational rules of Arabic and Quranic recitation.

Tafseer and Tajweed

Deepen your understanding of the Quran with Tafseer (interpretation) and learn proper pronunciation and rules of recitation through Takweed.

Hadith and Duas

Explore the teachings of the Prophet (PBUH) through Hadith and learn essential daily Duas to enrich your spiritual life.

Namaz Learning (Salat Guide)

Learn the correct method of performing Namaz (Salat) with step-by-step guidance, ensuring your prayers are done correctly.

Parental Guidance in Islamic Education

We provide resources and support for parents to actively participate in their children's Islamic education and Quran learning journey.
